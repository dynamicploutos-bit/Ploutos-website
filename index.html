Ongoing Project: PLSCO

What‚Äôs PLSCO?

PLSCO stands for Ploutos Local Supply Chain Optimization. It is our flagship project designed to transform how local vendors, producers, and communities manage logistics.

The Problem

Local vendors and producers face inefficiencies in distribution, poor inventory management, high costs, and limited market access. These challenges waste resources, reduce profits, and hinder community growth.

The Solution

PLSCO applies computer science ,data analytics, and cloud systems to predict demand, optimize routes, ensure secure transactions, and empower youth enterprises with scalable logistics.

Impact

Vendors save money, communities gain reliable access to goods, and the local economy becomes more resilient. PLSCO is empowerment through technology.
